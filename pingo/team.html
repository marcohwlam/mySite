Car Tuning

I upgraded my car's intake system. Everytime I go out and drive, I logged all the sensors' data, and used that to tune my car's ECU. By tweaking open loop and close loop A/F ratio, ignition timming, VTC, i-Vtec timing and etc, I am able to gain 15% of power just by software change!
